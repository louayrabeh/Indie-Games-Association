Comic Con 2017

 July, 7th, 8th, 9th 2017 Comic Con Tunisia came back with 2nd edition at the Kram exhibition park. The event that is dedicated for the geeks, mangakas, anime lovers, cosplayers and pop culture and comic books fans but also digital arts, video games and gaming.

 Indie Games Association was present with several virtual reality games which attracted the attendants of the event that were also amazed by the different projects and video games created within the association.

 For a first time in an event as enormous as the Comic Con, It was a huge success for IGA's exhibition stand as it attracted not only young people but parents too. And if anything, it shows the success of the goals of the association which are supporting and training students in science and technology, as well as encouraging them to use video games as a platform for their research and projects.
