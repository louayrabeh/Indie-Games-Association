Comic Con Tunisia 2018

 Comic Con is a multi-genre event that may include primarily comic books and science fiction/fantasy related film, television, and similar popular arts, but also a larger range of pop culture and entertainment elements across virtually all genres, including horror, Western animation, cosplaying, gaming, anime, manga, toys, collectibles and even Game Dev.

  Comic Con Tunisia 3rd edition was held July, 6th, 7th and 8th in the Kram's exhibition park, which included several activities such as cosplaying competitions, conferences, gaming tournaments and multidisciplinary workshops.

 Indie Games Association, like the previous edition, was present in Comic Con to showcase the newest games which were created by our developers and we also had a Game Dev workshop hosted by one of our members which was greatly admired by the attendants.
