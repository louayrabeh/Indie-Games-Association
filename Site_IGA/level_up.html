Level Up ISAMM

Level Up ISAMM is a series of workshops organised by Indie Games Association in partnership with ISAMM Events that took place at ISAMM. Its purpose was to prepare final year students for their graduation projects from the phase of brainstorming and thinking of the ideas until the deposit of the final report.

The workshops contained multiple topics such as :

  • fingerprint Brainstorming.
  • face Character design.
  • accessibility 3D modeling and sculpting.
  • directions_run Animating.
  • border_color Graphic design.
  • palette UI/UX design.
  • favorite Gameplay.
  • casino Level design.
  • music_note Sound engineering.
  • build Tools programming.
The event was hosted by competent instructors and the students were followed by our qualified coaches in the different stages of the production their projects.
